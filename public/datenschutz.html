Privacy Policy

Overview

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Stadt Land Fluss online game.

Data Collection

Personal Data

We do not collect or store any personal data such as names, email addresses, or other personally identifiable information.

Technical Data

We collect minimal technical data necessary for the game to function:

  • Language preference (stored locally in your browser)
  • Temporary game state during active games (stored locally and deleted when games end)
  • Anonymized server logs for security and error analysis

Cookies and Local Storage

We use local storage (not cookies) to save your language preference and temporary game data. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

We use CCM19 for cookie consent management. Please refer to their privacy policy for information about their data processing.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties. All game data remains on your device.

Data Retention

Game data is automatically deleted when you close the game or when games end. Language preferences remain until you clear your browser data.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, most data protection rights do not apply. You can clear your browser's local storage at any time to remove all game-related data.
