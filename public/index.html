Data Storage Information

This game uses minimal technical storage required for functionality:

  • Language Preference: Your selected language is stored locally to remember your choice
  • Game Data: Temporary game state is stored locally during active games and automatically cleared when games end
  • IP Geolocation: We use your IP address only to detect your country for initial language selection. No location data is stored

We do not use tracking cookies or store any personal information. All data remains on your device and is not shared with third parties.
